Our Story

A Bakery That
Truly Cares

At Bakes & Sweets, every cake is a labour of love. We're Rohtak's go-to destination for fully customized, 100% eggless rich cream cakes — perfect for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and every celebration that deserves something truly special.

Conveniently located at the 4 Puliya intersection on Sonipat Road, right next to Domino's, we're easy to find and always ready to serve. From classic flavours to bespoke designs, every creation leaves our kitchen with a promise of quality and joy.
