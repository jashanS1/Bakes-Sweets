Our Story

At Bakes & Sweets, we believe that every celebration deserves something truly special. Located in the heart of Rohtak, right next to Domino's at the 4 Puliya intersection, we have built a reputation for crafting moments of joy.

Our unique selling point is our fully customized, 100% eggless rich cream cakes, perfect for birthdays, weddings, and any special event. We pour love into every recipe, ensuring that every bite is as beautiful as it is delicious.
